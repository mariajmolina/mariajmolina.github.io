Group Training

In addition to research check-ins and discussions, our research group sets time aside for professional skills training. Studies have shown that career success can be attributed to factors beyond technical and scientific skillsets, specifically, professional skills, such as networking and time management [e.g., link]. Biweekly meetings will be held to help address this critically important training, along with other needed skills for careers after graduate school.
